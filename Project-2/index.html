Make Your Own Elf On The Shelf

This holiday project is fun and easy and even done with the family. I chose this project because the holidays are coming up

List of Materials:

  • 50mm Polystyrene ball
  • Skewer
  • Pink and white paint
  • Blue, black and red fine nib marker pens
  • Brown felt
  • Scissors
  • Glue
  • Red and white self adhesive felt
  • Penscil
  • Ruler
  • 8cm Cardboard tube
  • Red pipe cleaners
  • Stapler

How to make your cardboard elf:

  • Place the ball on a skewer and paint with a coat of pink paint. Leave to dry. Next, paint two white eyes on the pink ball and leave to dry.
  • Cut out some brown felt hair and stick it to the top of the head with glue
  • For the hat, cut out a semi-circle of red self adhesive felt. Peel off the backing paper, then roll into a cont and stick to top of head. Draw and cut a band of white self adhesive felt and stick around the base of the hat.
  • Measure and cut the red pipe cleaners into two 10cm long arms and two 20cm long legs. Cut out some white flet mittents and stick to the ends of each arm.
  • Take the cardboard tube and staple the arms to the top, and legs to the bottom. Measure and cut a piece of red self adhesive felt to stick around the tube, covering the staples.
  • Draw and cut a zig-zag strip of white felt and stick around the collar.
  • Glue the elf's head to his body, and leave to dry.
